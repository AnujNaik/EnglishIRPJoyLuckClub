My Story

Hey there, I'm Jing-mei Woo, but I prefer to be called June. Not that I don't like my Chinese name or anything, it's just that- nevermind. I was born in ___ in San Francsico to my loving, yet overstepping, parents who immigrated from China. I grew up surrounded by a Chinese society despite living in America, and I dropped out of college later to pursue copywriting. Learn more about my interests below. Just don't get too psyched, I'm not the most interesting or successful person ever.

My Home

This is the location of where I was born and raised: San Francisco. My mother, Suyuan Woo, originally lived in Kweilin following the Sino-Japanese War. Once the war in China became too dangerous, my mom left Kweilin, her husband, property, and babies to Chungking where she met her current husband, and my dad, Canning Woo. From there, they eventually immigrated to San Francisco.

My Interests and Passions

As a child, my mother pushed me to do many things like playing the piano, being a prodigy, and even imitating Shirley Temple! Now that I'm an adult, I have a few more "refined" interests which you can check out below.

Mahjong

Though I'm pretty bad at the Chinese version, I love playing Mahjong with my Jewish friends. I hope to get better and compete against my aunts in the Joy Luck Club!
